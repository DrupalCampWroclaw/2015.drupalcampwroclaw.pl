DrupalCamp Wrocław 2015 is a conference organized for Drupal community and others interested in the software. For three days (Friday, Saturday and Sunday), there will be lectures and workshops given by the Drupal experts. The conference is free to attend.

We organize the DrupalCamp Wrocław conference for the fourth time encouraged by the success of last editions. There were lots of lecturers as well as those who wanted to hear the lectures and sponsors. A big dose of knowledge along with the openness let the participants spend their time in great atmosphere. We have received a great response from programmers and users; that is why we have decided to double the number of volunteers and prepare additional offers.

DrupalCamp Wrocław is a international conference. In 2014 we had speakers from Germany, Sweden, Belgium, Finland, UK and Poland.
